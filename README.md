# West Technology Group (west)
West Technology Group is a cloud-based global technology company providing customer experience and communications solutions through its WestCX platform. The company operates through two main brands: Mosaicx, which delivers AI-native conversational automation for enterprise customer engagement, and TeleVox, which provides digital patient engagement solutions for healthcare organizations including providers, payers, and life sciences companies.

## Features

| Name | Description |
|------|-------------|
| WestCX Orchestrate | AI-native platform coordinating customer interactions across voice, SMS, RCS, chat, and email channels with intelligent routing and automation. |
| WestCX Engage | Real-time AI engagement platform combining LinguaAI, journey orchestration, and Insights360 analytics into a unified patient interaction ecosystem. |
| WestCX Outreach | Proactive outreach automation delivering personalized voice, SMS, email, and RCS messages for appointment reminders, health campaigns, and alerts. |
